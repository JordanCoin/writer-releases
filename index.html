Writer

A macOS writing app with an AI editorial witness

Download for macOS Requires macOS 15 or later

Not a ghost-writer — a thoughtful companion that observes, reflects, and proposes. Every edit requires your consent. No silent modifications. No surprises. You remain the author.

What Writer Does

Observe

Claude watches you write, noticing what's working and what isn't — without interrupting your flow.

Reflect

Ask questions that help you see your work differently. Find the voice, the threads, the possibilities.

Propose

When you're ready, Claude suggests changes. You see the original, the suggestion, and why — then you decide.

Disappear

Beautiful typography that fades while you write. A warm, distraction-free space for thinking.
